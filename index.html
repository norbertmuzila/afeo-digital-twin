Crop Monitoring Module

Real-time NDVI time series, phenological stage tracking, and crop classification maps from Sentinel-2 and MODIS. Historical anomaly detection across all monitored agricultural zones worldwide.

🧪

Soil Analysis Module

Soil moisture maps from SMAP and Sentinel-1 SAR. Texture classification, organic carbon estimation, and nutrient deficiency detection for precision irrigation scheduling.

🏜️

Drought Monitor

Combined SPI, SPEI, and VCI drought indices. Flash drought detection, seasonal forecasting, and impact assessment on agriculture and water supply. 3 active severe drought alerts across the Horn of Africa.

⚠️

Early Warning System

Predictive analytics combining satellite EO, weather models, market prices, and conflict data. 90-day food security forecasts with IPC-compatible classifications for humanitarian response planning.

📋

Reports & Analytics

Generate custom reports for any region, time period, and thematic area. Export as PDF, CSV, or GeoTIFF. Automated weekly briefings for government agencies and humanitarian organizations.

⚙️

Platform Settings

Manage satellite data sources, alert thresholds, notification preferences, user roles, and API integrations. Configure custom NDVI thresholds for your specific crop types and regions.
